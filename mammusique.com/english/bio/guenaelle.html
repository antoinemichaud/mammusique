She combines the mildness of Anjou where she was born with the stronger character of Périgord where she now lives. By turns a singer and a comedian she also plays different instruments.Her motto on stage as well as in life: generosity, pleasure and commitment.

• Is now performing in 4 shows : "Brassens, passionnément", "Jazz Cabaret", "Songs & Humor Cabaret", "Cannelle introduces Mary Poppins" (for children).
• On stage she has interpreted among others Brecht, Weill, Satie, Gershwin, Louki, Nougaro, Piaf... and has also gone from musical comedy to film music.
• Street performer at times when she takes part in clown or ball sculpture shows.
