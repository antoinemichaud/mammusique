He left Quebec as a kid and ever since has taken his violin across continents and into music academies, catching an alto on the way, africanizing his classical music and jazzifying his folk.

• CDs and several tours with Cordacor’ (3 albums), Quatuor IXI (2), Artango, Abed Azrié (2), Stéphane Delicq (3), Jean-Philippe Watremez, Thomas Pitiot (2), La Rasbaïa, Thomasi (3), Tavagna, Gilliane Kim (2), Kelly Family...
• Has worked with Francis Lemarque, François Castiello, Matmatah, Salvatore Adamo, Bénabar, Bruno Girard, Mama Kaya (3), Latcho Drom, Riccardo Del Fra, Antoine Hervé, Dominique Pifarély, Louis Sclavis, Jean-Pascal Boffo (2), Alifair (2), François Raullin, Paddy Kelly, Celtic Sailors, Manau...
• Compositions for theater and cinema.
