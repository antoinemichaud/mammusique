Who would be surprised that a musician claiming French, Quebec and NewYork backgrounds be a singer, a saxophonist, a pianist, an arranger, a programmer, a sound engineer and a director.

Yet we are surprised indeed by his exceptional and mature musicality.

• Concerts in the US with his own bands : Slant, Astroglide, Honey and the Bees.
• Composer/director for Sean Lennon, Jennifer Lopez, Warner Brothers, Toots and the Maytals, Willie Nelson, Lucy Woodward, Ben Harper, Washington Heights film soundtrack, MAM (5 albums)…
Composed the song for the United Nations’ charter.
• Image compositions : Publishing Companies, Thundersky Pictures, Bay Networks, Motorola, Dragon Software, Apple Computers, Siemens… Show and event compositions : Media 100, Videonics, Electric Image.
