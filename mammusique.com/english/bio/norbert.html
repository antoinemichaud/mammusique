This precocious virtuoso, a prize winner of music academy awards and others, has already played with first-class innovative jazz

musicians on the world scene. Brilliant vibraphone player, amazing human beatboxer, astonishing on keyboards.

• CDs and tours with his own bandsfeaturing, among others, the following musicians : Julien Lourau, Patrice Soletti, Karim Ziad, Bruno Chevillon, Nicolas Mahieux…some of them having won awards such as a nomination at « Django d’Or » or a prize at « the Festival Musicolor d’Évry ».
• CDs, tours on the jazz scene with General Elektriks, Groove Gang, Urban Mood, Méga Octet, Jaleo, Quoi de Neuf Docteur, the Marc Berthoumieux and Louis Winsberg' quintets, and the bands of Sylvain Kassap, Yves Robert, Guillaume Orti, Henri Texier, Thierry Fanfan…; also French and international varieties with Thomas Fersen, Corine, Djamel Ben Yelles and Cheb Moumen…
• Plays and in some cases composes for animated cartoons and short films.
